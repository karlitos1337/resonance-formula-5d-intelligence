## VI. Limitations & Caveats

### Simulation Limitations
1. **Simplified agents**: Real humans are more complex
2. **Linear payoffs**: Real consequences often have thresholds and nonlinearities
3. **No external shocks**: Real systems face unexpected crises
4. **Perfect information transfer**: Real communication is lossy
5. **No strategic deception**: Real actors may game the system

### However:
The qualitative patterns (open wins, closed fails) are likely **robust** across more complex models because they reflect fundamental principles of information flow and adaptation.
